About Us

Built for businesses that are getting left behind by modern tech.

I've spent the last 15 years in enterprise technology, helping build and scale companies from early stage to billion-dollar outcomes. I've been in the room where decisions are made to automate support, increase pricing, and prioritize only the largest customers.

That model works for enterprise. It breaks small and mid-sized businesses.

If you're not spending $100K or more a year, you're not getting real support. You're getting a login and a queue. And the reality is, those dollars matter more to your business than they ever will to those vendors.

At the same time, the pace of technology is accelerating. AI, automation, integrations, data. It's creating massive opportunity, but also widening the gap between companies with dedicated IT teams and those without. Most small businesses are being forced to figure this out on their own.

That's the problem. OS Intelligence is the answer.

We take the same principles, tools, and strategies used inside high-growth technology companies and apply them in a way that actually works for your business. No bloated tech stacks. No "figure it out yourself" platforms. No support tickets that go nowhere.

Instead, we act as your operating partner. We design, implement, and manage the systems your business runs on, tailored to how you actually operate. Human-first, practical, and built to drive outcomes, not just adoption.

This isn't another vendor relationship. This is a partner who sits on your side of the table.
